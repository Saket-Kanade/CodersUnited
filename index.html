What would you like to become?

Take a look at all the different careers we can prepare you for.

Software Developer

Software development students can strengthen their skills in the developing, designing, and programming involved in creating and maintaining applications.

Learn: Javascript, Bootstrap, Atom...

Quality Assurance Specialist

Quality assurance is a way of preventing mistakes and defects in manufactured products and avoiding problems when delivering products or services to customers.

Learn: Selenium, Java, RestAPI ...

Web Developer

Do you ever look at a website and wonder how you could do it better? Explore the work involved in developing a website ranging from a single static page to a fully functional dynamic website.

Learn: jQuery, AngularJs, HTML/CSS...

I.T. Specialist

Do you want to be a critical part of business operations and have enough coding experience to blow the CTO away? Try our information technology course to learn how to use computers to store, retrieve, transmit, and interpret computer data.

Cybersecurity Engineer

Learn how to protect computer systems and networks from the theft or damage of hardware, software or electronic data with our cyber security program. In your learnings, you will become an expert in the latest cyber security tools that will be sure to jumpstart your career in this field.

Database Developer

Obtaining real world requirements, analyzing them, designing data and system functions, as well as implementing these operations; these are all things that are throughly explained through our Database development course. Your Coders United training will dive into the latest tools in database development that will be sure to set you up for success.
