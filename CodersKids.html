What is CodersKids?

At Coders United we believe that every child should have the opportuinity to thrive in any given field. Due to COVID-19 a lot of schools and universities are forced to shift to an online learning platform. We are trying to formalize a process to fill this knowledge gap starting with our great development team but extending to all facets of education. To fill this gap we are providing FREE web development courses to students between the ages of 12 to 20. This will help them hone their skills and explore an avenue for a career in Computer Science. With our highly motivated team of lesson planners, educators and software developers we are hoping to commit to a 6 month process to teach students professional web development at an indepth level.
